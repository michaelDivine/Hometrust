About

Hometrust helps parties create and service installment sales agreements for residential real estate transactions. Our model uses standardized agreements, automation via Ricardian contracts, and trust law to help sellers self-fund the sale of their home without the need for bank lending. This results in higher returns for sellers and lower costs for buyers.

In short, we make the process of buying and selling a home easier by empowering you.

Launching Summer of 2019.
